About Me

Hello! My name is Danielle Onwuka and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web design started back when I was in high school and coded my first HTML page. I took a bit of a detour in college and graduated with two degrees in Art and Graphic Design. Due to this I remembered what I loved about web design and computers.
Fast-forward to today where I've rediscovered my love of coding. My main focus is to learn as much as I can build accessible, inclusive products and digital experiences.
